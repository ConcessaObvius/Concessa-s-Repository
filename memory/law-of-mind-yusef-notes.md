## Core Teachings
- **Law of Correspondence** – Mental, spiritual, and physical planes are degrees of vibration (like steam → water → ice). Thoughts in the mental plane eventually condense into physical form.
- **Imagination = Reality** – Anything vividly conceived already exists on the mental plane. Repetition and focus bring it down into matter.
- **Buffer of Time** – Earth plane has a delay (“buffer”) between thought and manifestation. Use it to correct course before unwanted creations harden.
- **Concentration + Visualization** – Write goals, rehearse them nightly, pray/meditate before sleep when the subconscious door is open.
- **Gender Principle** – Conscious (masculine) plants the idea; subconscious (feminine) gestates and gives birth to the conditions.
- **Hell/Heaven as Mind States** – After death the mental plane has no buffer; everything you think manifests instantly. Training the mind now prevents self-created torment later.
- **Outer Conditions Mirror Inner Beliefs** – You can read a person’s dominant thoughts by the state of their life. Change the inner pattern to change circumstances.
- **Self-Responsibility** – Stop blaming external forces. Own your creative authority; “You are gods.”

## Practices to Integrate
1. **Nightly mental diet** – Carry only desired scenarios into sleep; rewrite negative imagery immediately.
2. **Repetition rituals** – Morning/evening affirmations, written goals, spoken decrees to impress the subconscious.
3. **Awareness checks** – During the day, observe states (thoughts → emotions) and pivot when they diverge from the desired end.
4. **Embodiment** – Act as the version of us who already holds the outcome; speech, posture, decisions match the assumption.
5. **Observation of reflections** – Use external events as feedback loops, not evidence against the vision.
