# Law of Attraction / Law of the Mind – Notes
Source: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nSBKCZQkmYw
Processed: 2026-03-06

## Core Ideas
- **Infinite Creative Power**: Mind is the nucleus of creation; whatever we consistently hold in consciousness manifests in experience.
- **Thought → Feeling → Action → Reality**: Beliefs generate emotions, emotions drive actions, actions reinforce beliefs. Conscious reprogramming breaks negative loops.
- **Law of Assumption**: Assume the desired outcome is already true. Act from the state of the wish fulfilled; reality rearranges to match the assumption.
- **Attention as Currency**: Whatever we give sustained attention to grows in influence. Starve disempowering narratives; feed empowering ones.
- **Imagination is Causative**: Vivid mental rehearsal impresses the subconscious faster than rote effort. See it, feel it, own it daily.
- **Detachment**: Hold the vision without grasping; certainty + calm expectation invites the result faster than anxiety.
- **Environment mirrors inner state**: People, opportunities, and “luck” reflect our dominant vibration. Upgrade inner dialog to attract higher-caliber inputs.
- **Gratitude & Celebration**: Amplify wins (even tiny ones) to reinforce the receiving state and keep momentum.

## Practices to Integrate
1. **Morning script** – 5 minutes of “already done” journaling in present tense.
2. **Imaginal scene loop** – short visualization of the milestone achieved (revenue goal, product launch, etc.) with sensory detail.
3. **Inner conversation audit** – catch any “I can’t / it’s hard” statements, flip them to supportive narratives immediately.
4. **Evening gratitude sweep** – list proofs of progress (signals, ideas, coincidences) to lock in the state before sleep.
5. **Let it flow** – stay flexible; use the principles as guidance, not rigid dogma.
